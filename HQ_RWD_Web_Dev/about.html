Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Brewed with Heart

"Every cup of HQ White Coffee carries the warmth of generations — from the charcoal-roasted beans of Ipoh to the hands that brew them with care."

Our Journey

The Beginning

A Humble Start

HQ White Coffee began as a small neighborhood café, serving freshly brewed coffee alongside traditional kaya toast, simple noodles, and classic Chinese dishes.

The Growth

A Growing Community

As more customers discovered and fell in love with our food, the café quickly grew in popularity. With increasing support from the community, we expanded our offerings and space.

Today

A Modern Dining Experience

Today, HQ White Coffee has evolved into a full restaurant offering a diverse menu — alongside our signature local favorites, we now serve Western cuisine, Hainanese specialties, and Italian pasta.
